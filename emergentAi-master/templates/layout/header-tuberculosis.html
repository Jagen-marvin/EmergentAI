ML-aided Tuberculosis screening

Our deep learning-based tuberculosis screening application uses AI to analyze chest X-ray images and assist radiologists in detecting possible cases of tuberculosis. By helping to identify TB more accurately and efficiently, our technology has the potential to improve the early detection and treatment of this disease.
