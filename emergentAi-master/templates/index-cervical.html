Empowering Healthcare with AI-Powered Cervical Cancer Screening

This is an integrated cervical cancer screening platform with AI-based decision-making support for health care practitioners. When used in conjunction with mobile colposcopy, it provides AI-based screening of pre-cancerous lesions within seconds at an accuracy of > 90%, a sensitivity of 98% and a specificity of 82%.

It leverages AI-based, computer vision techniques to provide decision support tools for healthcare practitioners involved in cervical cancer screening using the Visual Inspection with Acetic acid (VIA) approach.

AI-based support tools include lesion segmentation, cervix location, lesion quadrant prediction, and flagging cases that are suspicious for precancerous lesions. This point of care component of technology is deployed on a sophisticated android mobile application.

This platform is not a replacement for a health care provider's clinical judgment; rather, it is an additional support tool for cancer screening assessments thereby reducing the possibility of human error and shortening turn-around time.
