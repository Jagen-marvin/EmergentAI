Our Projects

Cervical cancer screening platform

Our cervical cancer screening platform, Cacx, is a mobile application that utilizes the power of machine learning to provide fast, accurate, and non-invasive screening results for women. With its advanced algorithms and user-friendly interface, Cacx helps healthcare providers detect cervical cancer early and improve the lives of women.

Learn more

ML-aided tuberculosis screening

Our deep learning-powered tuberculosis screening solution utilizes advanced computer vision techniques to accurately detect TB in chest x-rays, offering a fast and non-invasive screening method that greatly improves the efficiency and accuracy of TB diagnoses for healthcare providers and patients alike.

Learn more

Yogera application

The Yogera application is a transformative common voice platform designed to support the Luganda local language. Yogera enables developers to build accurate natural language processing (NLP) models by collecting and utilizing an extensive and diverse range of text-audio data contributed by a global community of speakers.
